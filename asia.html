A large continent filled with different locations to see; Containing a rich history and nature. Asia has a little bit of everything to offer, including many beautiful summer spots.
         
 
 

Southeast Asia is the perfect area for a summer visit. In particular, Vietnam, Thailand and Malaysia are some of the most popular spots in the region due to their nightlife, natural beauty and diversity. And the best part is that they are all accessible by train! Here are some of the best touristic attraction to visit:

  • Hoi An Ancient Town (Vietnam)

  • The Grand Palace (Thailand)

  • Escape Penang (Malaysia)

  • Halong Bay (Vietnam)

  • Batu Caves (Malaysia)

  • War Remnants Museum (Vietnam)

  • Siam Paragon (Thailand)

  • And of course, the cities of Bangkok, Hanoi and Kuala Lumpur, all featuring and amazing nightlife
