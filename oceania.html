A sanctuary of islands and aquatic life, Oceania is the ideal tropical paradise to go diving and experience the mysterious world underwater.

   
         
    
 

Although Australia and New Zealand make up the spotlight, there are many other countries in Oceania which are worth visiting, along with some exciting attractions:

  • Garden of the Sleeping Giant (Fiji)

  • To Sua Ocean Trench (Samoa)

  • Mount Tavurvur (Papua New Guinea)

  • Anahalu Cave (Tonga)

  • Matira Beach (French Polynesia)

  • Amedee Lighthouse (New Caledonia)

  • Jellyfish Lake (Palau)

  • Kepirohi Waterfall (Micronesia)
